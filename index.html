About Me: A Journey of Passion and Purpose

I grew up in New Delhi, where curiosity and a love for learning shaped my early years. My fascination with technology began when I received my first laptop and deepened after I built my own PC. In 9th grade, I discovered HTML and CSS, sparking my interest in coding and creating digital experiences that bring ideas to life.


I pursued a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) from Maharaja Surajmal Institute of Technology, affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University. During this time, I focused on web development, mastering frameworks like Django and Flask. A key project was building a sentiment analysis web portal with my team, where we overcame challenges integrating machine learning with the frontend an experience that strengthened my skills and passion for innovative tech solutions.


Currently, I am pursuing a Master's in Computer Applications with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML Honors) at Lovely Professional University, Phagwara. This program has expanded my expertise in data analysis, visualization, IoT, and deep learning, helping me develop strong problem-solving and analytical abilities while reinforcing my belief in ethical, data-driven innovation.


Beyond academics, I love exploring new places, diving into manga and anime, gaming, and discovering new music. These interests keep me inspired, creative, and balanced. As a fresh post graduate standing at the intersection of experience and ambition, I'm eager to contribute to impactful AI and technology projects through internships or full-time roles. My journey has taught me that success isn't just about achievements but about the value we create and the lives we influence.
